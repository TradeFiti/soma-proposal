Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the build take?
It depends on the package. Foundation takes approximately 6 weeks, Growth takes around 14 weeks, and Scale is a 20-week build. We work in agile sprints with weekly demos so you see progress from day one.
Do I own the code?
Yes, 100%. Full source code is transferred to you on completion. You're never locked in — the technology is yours to keep, modify, or hand off to another team if you ever choose to.
What about hosting costs?
Included in the monthly fee. We handle everything — servers, security updates, backups, SSL certificates, and performance monitoring. You focus on teaching.
Can I start small and upgrade?
Absolutely. Start with Foundation and add features as you grow. Every package is designed to build on the last, so upgrading is seamless with no wasted work.
What if I need changes after launch?
Monthly support includes up to 10 hours of changes and improvements. That covers bug fixes, content updates, minor feature tweaks, and performance optimization.
How do you handle payments from different countries?
We integrate Stripe for global card payments, M-Pesa for East African mobile money, and manual invoicing for corporate clients. Your students can pay however is most convenient for them.
