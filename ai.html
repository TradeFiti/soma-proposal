What SomaAI Can Do

Feature Highlights

🗣️

Conversation Practice

Real-time AI conversations on 50+ topics — from everyday small talk to advanced business discussions. Students speak naturally and build confidence.

📝

Grammar Correction

Instant, gentle corrections with clear explanations. The AI highlights errors without breaking the flow of conversation, so students learn naturally.

🎤

Pronunciation

Voice analysis with native speaker comparison. Students hear the difference, practice specific sounds, and track their pronunciation improvement over time.

🎭

Scenario Practice

Restaurant ordering, job interviews, business meetings, doctor visits — realistic role-plays that prepare students for real-world English situations.

📈

Adaptive Difficulty

AI adjusts to each student's level automatically. Beginners get simpler vocabulary and slower pacing, while advanced learners face complex idioms and nuance.

📊

Tutor Reports

Detailed AI practice reports sent to the assigned tutor — covering weak areas, practice frequency, and improvement trends so tutors can personalize lessons.
